Khoi Vinh on weighing Sketch vs Photoshop.

I’ve been wondering if there was a trend to move away from Adobe’s monolithic design suite to smaller, cheaper, nimbler applications on the Mac. I’ve been using Pixelmator almost exclusively for years now, and I’ve been eyeing Sketch as an Illustrator replacement.

It looks like other folks are having the same idea.

If you’re designing for the web and not print, and as long as you have no dependencies on creative agencies, I see very little reason to continue propping up Adobe’s monopoly.

Of course, you shouldn’t be designing web pages in an image editor at all, you should use HTML and CSS until you’ve settled out the layout and the typography. “Web designers” who deliver Photoshop documents are liars and thieves.

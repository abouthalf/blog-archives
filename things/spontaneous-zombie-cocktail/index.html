Spontaneous Zombie Cocktail

Things
Sunday, July 1st, 2007
Tweet

I’m home on a Sunday, doing the laundry, and thanks to the OLC I can’t get a homemade martini so what to do….Spontaneous Zombie Cocktail!

  • 1 oz spiced rum
  • 1/2 oz Triple Sec
  • 1/2 oz Blue Curacao
  • Dash of Bitters
  • Orange Juice
  • Squeeze of lime

Pour the rum, triple sec, and Blue Curacao over ice in a collins glass or a tumbler. Fill with orange juice. Add a dash of bitters, and a squeeze of lime.
